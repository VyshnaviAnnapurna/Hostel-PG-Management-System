Hostel/PG Management System


The Hostel / PG Management System is a web-based application
developed to simplify and automate the daily administrative tasks
of hostel or paying guest accommodations. Traditional hostel
management systems rely heavily on manual record keeping,
which is time-consuming, error-prone, and difficult to maintain.
This project aims to replace the manual system with an efficient,
reliable, and user-friendly digital solution.
